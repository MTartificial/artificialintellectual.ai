§ 04

About

Someone using AI as a genuine thinking partner — across investing, building tools, and working through ideas. The gap between having an idea and shipping something has narrowed considerably, and this site is an attempt to document what that looks like in practice. The goal is financial independence, approached the same way everything else here is: with honesty about where things stand, clarity about the direction, and a bias toward doing over theorizing.

Artificialintellectual.ai is a living record of what it looks like to use these tools seriously — the wins, the wrong turns, and the things still being figured out.
